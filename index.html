Main Story

In the year 2245, humanity has reached new heights in space exploration. Astronaut Diguzma, a seasoned explorer with a reputation for getting the toughest jobs done, is on the most critical mission of his life. He is alone, floating in the endless reaches of space, surrounded by five mysterious planets. These planets, hidden from Earth's view, hold secrets that could reshape humanity’s understanding of the universe.

But Diguzma can’t do this alone. His mission is to uncover the truth behind these planets' atmospheres, surfaces, and hidden treasures. That's where you come in. As the Earth-bound operator, it’s your job to guide Diguzma through a series of scientific tests. Together, you'll analyze the planets' atmospheres, map their surfaces, and reveal their true nature. Your choices will determine the success of the mission, and the future of space discovery.

Project Goal

Our mission is to make learning about exoplanets an exciting and immersive experience. Through our game, players will explore distant worlds, conducting real scientific tests alongside our astronaut character. By solving cosmic mysteries and uncovering new planets, players will gain knowledge about exoplanets in a fun and indirect way. Join us in the adventure beyond the stars and learn while you play!
